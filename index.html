M. Udara Peiris

I am an Associate Professor of Financial Economics in the Department of Economics at Oberlin College, Ohio, USA.

I am also a Visiting Researcher at the National Bank of Slovakia and a Global Academic Fellow at Verite Research, Sri Lanka.

My research focuses on the nexus between the macroeconomy and financial markets, with emphasis on monetary policy, capital flows, and sovereign debt. I completed my PhD and MSc (Dist.) in Financial Economics at the University of Oxford as a Clarendon Scholar.
