UP

M. Udara Peiris

I am an Associate Professor of Financial Economics in the Department of Economics at Oberlin College, Ohio, USA.

I am also a Visiting Researcher at the National Bank of Slovakia and a Global Academic Fellow at Verite Research, Sri Lanka.

My research examines monetary policy, international capital flows, and sovereign debt. Before joining Oberlin, I completed my PhD and MSc (Distinction) in Financial Economics at the University of Oxford as a Clarendon Scholar.
